Privacy & Terms

1. Email Privacy

Your email address is never visible to other users. You are identified only by your 8-digit Member ID. Email is used solely for login authentication and is accessible only to you and authorized administrators.

2. Message Privacy

Messages are stored securely in Firebase Firestore. Only the sender and recipient can read a conversation under normal circumstances.

3. Administrator Access

Authorized administrators may access conversation content strictly when required for:

  • Customer support and dispute resolution
  • Abuse, harassment, or threat investigations
  • Legal compliance and court orders
  • Platform safety and security
  • Government or law enforcement requests as required by applicable law

4. Audit Logging

All administrator access is logged automatically including: Admin ID, affected User ID, action performed, and timestamp. Reports are handled confidentially — the reporter's identity is never disclosed to the reported member.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your account and messages. Contact support via your Member ID.

Community Guidelines

SM 10X is built on respect and safety. All members must follow these guidelines:

✅ Allowed

  • Respectful personal and professional communication
  • Sharing information, support, or collaboration
  • Reporting abusive behavior using the 🚩 Report button

❌ Not Allowed

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
  • Threats of violence or harm
  • Spam or unsolicited promotions
  • Scamming, phishing, or fraud

  • Impersonation of other members or administrators

Reporting

If you experience any violation, tap the 🚩 Report icon in any chat. Reports are reviewed confidentially by our admin team. Admin can warn, mute, suspend, or permanently ban violators.
